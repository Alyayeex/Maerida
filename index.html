Maerida

Embark on a Thrilling Journey in the World Maerida.

Start Exploring

Lore

Maerida is a world full of stories, collected and narrated in 'Books'. Discover each book and its 'Chapters' as well as the various 'Quests' to be found in each region.

Go Explore

Characters

Stories contain figures and there is just way too many of them! Luckily you can discover and meet all of them and the remarkable tales they have to tell.

From Bloodphantoms to the most powerful Magia Lord Pendalus, they have a unique story.

Read More

Dragons

"The heart of Maerida is where dragons arise. In the heart of dragons, Maerida lies."

Being one of the first primal creatures, they roam the world, leaving tales and myths behind. Meet these glorious creatures and find out where to find them.

Discover

Fauna & Flora

A world wouldn't thrive long without a proper environment. Every continent and region has its own unique set of flora and fauna.

This is essential knowledge and worth to check out if you want to navigate with ease through the world and not miss a single leaf!

Guides

To help you even more, there are dozen of guides on a global or regional level that can help you out on your quest.

And for the excited and curious fantasy lovers, yes, there is a guide on 'How to start world-building' so you can go and turn that imagination of yours into "somewhat of a" reality!
