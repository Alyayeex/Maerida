On Maerida you will find a total of five continents, listed down below. This section will give you a short description and a little insight on the unique areas of each continent.

Click on the continents to read a description and discover their regions.

Elkuria, a captivating continent inspired by the Middle East, unveils a land of golden deserts, nomadic tribes, and majestic Moths. Rich in resources, spices, and secrets, Elkuria's allure beckons adventurers. Its trade with Niantur, a lush neighboring realm, weaves a tapestry of prosperity and intrigue. Step into Elkuria's embrace, where mystery and wonder flourish beneath the desert sun.

Feyratir is a realm steeped in enchantment, where magic flows through its captivating landscapes. Inspired by the tapestry of Celtic cultures, this mystical continent is adorned with ancient forests and inhabited by extraordinary creatures. The very air hums with the essence of magia, as ethereal energies entwine with the natural world.
