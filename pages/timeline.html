The Age of Fire is the first period of planet Maerida. It is said that the first creatures to appear on the planet where the white giants, but no living entity had ever witnessed them. Later this era, a continent called Niantur started to hold more different lives and dragons were born, first appearing in the burning vast lands called Ragaslinn. Niantur forms the heart of Maerida.

After the age of Fire, the first Magia started to form. They were called the Feyian people and settled themselves on the continent Feyratir with Kha'Krulvar as the capital city. They went through a golden time both economically and culturally.

The Magian Era marks the end of the Great Dragons and the start of a new generation Magia. A new continent has been discovered: Sterhalm, a group of floating rocks.
